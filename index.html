Concept & Inspiration

“Reignition” – Power Plant LED Installation Concept
Once a monument to industrial energy, the abandoned power plant becomes a living sculpture—its darkened shell reignited by thousands of pulsing LEDs. Reignition explores the tension between decay and renewal, channeling raw electricity into hypnotic patterns that respond to sound and movement.

As the structure breathes with light, ravers move in sync—bodies, rhythm, and code merging into one collective mechanism. Energy flows through wires, sound, and living bodies alike, dissolving the boundary between human and machine. The obsolete becomes vital again—a futuristic temple where techno reclaims space through vibration and light.

Technical Specs

  • Fully sound-reactive system
  • 6400 individually addressable LEDs
  • 450–500 meters of cabling
  • Customized sound-reactive scenes and effects
  • Compatible with Resolume and other mapping software via Artnet
